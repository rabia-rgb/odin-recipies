Chai

Description

Need a pick-me-up? A cup of spiced Pakistani Chai is just what you need!

Ingredients:

  • Water: 2 cups
  • Black Tea: 2 teaspoons loose black tea
  • Milk: 1 cup (whole milk is preferred for creaminess)
  • Sugar: 1-2 tablespoons (adjust to taste)
  • Ginger: 1-2 slices or 1/2 teaspoon grated
  • Cinnamon: a small stick (optional)

Steps:

  1. Boil Water: In a pot, bring water to a boil.
  2. Add Tea: Add tea leaves or tea bags and spices, if using. Let it simmer for 2-3 minutes.
  3. Add Milk: Pour in milk and let it boil again.
  4. Sweeten: Add sugar to taste and stir well.
  5. Strain and Serve: Strain the tea into cups and enjoy hot.
