biryani

Description:

Indulge in Pakistan’s pride—Chicken Hyderabadi Biryani, a fragrant blend of tender chicken and basmati rice, rich in flavor and tradition

Ingredients:

  • Chicken: 1kg
  • Basmati rice: 3 cups
  • Onions (fried): 2
  • Yogurt: 1 cup
  • Ginger-Garlic Paste: 2 tbsp
  • Green Chilies: 3-4 (Chopped)
  • Corriander and Mint leaves: 1/2 bunch each (chopped)
  • Spices

Steps

  1. Marinate Chicken: Combine chicken with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, spices, and salt. Let it sit for 30 minutes.
  2. Fry Onions: Sauté sliced onions in oil until golden brown. Set aside half for garnish.
  3. Cook Chicken: Add marinated chicken and chopped tomatoes to the pot; cook until chicken is tender.
  4. Boil Rice: Cook soaked basmati rice in boiling water until 70% done, then drain.
  5. Layer Biryani: In the chicken pot, layer half of the rice, followed by fried onions, coriander, and mint.
  6. Repeat Layers: Add the remaining rice and repeat with more onions, coriander, and mint.
  7. Dum Cooking: Cover tightly and cook on low heat for 20-30 minutes.
  8. Serve: Garnish with fried onions and serve hot with raita or salad.
