Sheer

Description:

Crave something sweet? Sheer Khurma’s creamy goodness with nuts will have you coming back for more

Ingredients:

  • Vermicelli: 1 cup
  • Milk: 4 cups
  • Sugar: 1/2 cup (adjust to taste)
  • Dates: 10-12 (chopped)
  • Almonds: 1/4 cup (sliced or chopped)
  • Pistachios: 1/4 cup (sliced or chopped)
  • Ghee or Oil: 2-3 tbsp (for frying)

Steps:

  1. Roast Vermicelli: In a pan, heat 1-2 tablespoons of ghee or oil. Add the vermicelli and roast until golden brown. Set aside.
  2. Prepare Nuts and Dates: In the same pan, add a little more ghee if needed and sauté the sliced almonds, pistachios, cashews, and raisins until lightly toasted. Add the chopped dates and mix well. Remove and set aside.
  3. Boil Milk: In a separate pot, bring the milk to a boil over medium heat.
  4. Add Sugar: Once the milk starts boiling, add sugar and stir until it dissolves completely.
  5. Combine Ingredients: Add the roasted vermicelli to the boiling milk and cook for about 5-7 minutes until the vermicelli is cooked through.
  6. Add Nuts and Dates: Stir in the sautéed nuts and dates, along with cardamom powder. Mix well.
  7. Simmer: Let the mixture simmer for another 5 minutes on low heat, allowing the flavors to meld.
  8. Serve: Remove from heat and let it cool slightly. Serve warm or chilled, garnished with extra nuts if desired.
